Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 27, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to BlockBuddies4Life's Privacy Policy. This policy describes how we handle information in relation to our gaming content creation services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect Directly

BlockBuddies4Life does not directly collect, store, or process personal information from viewers. We do not have:

  • User accounts or registration systems
  • Email collection forms
  • Cookies or tracking technologies on this website
  • Direct data collection mechanisms

2.2 Information Collected by Third-Party Platforms

When you view our content on third-party platforms (YouTube, TikTok), those platforms may collect information about you according to their own privacy policies. This may include:

  • Viewing history and watch time
  • Interactions (likes, comments, shares)
  • Device information and IP address
  • Location data (as permitted by platform settings)
  • Account information on those platforms

We do not have direct access to this information. It is governed by the privacy policies of each platform.

3. How We Use Platform APIs

We use official platform APIs (including TikTok API) to:

  • Upload and manage our video content
  • View aggregated analytics about our content performance
  • Schedule and publish content
  • Manage our channel presence

We access only the data necessary for content management and do not access individual viewer personal information through these APIs.

4. Third-Party Services

Our content is hosted and distributed through third-party platforms. Each has its own privacy policy:

4.1 YouTube

  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • YouTube collects data as part of the Google ecosystem
  • You can manage your YouTube privacy settings through your Google account

4.2 TikTok

  • Privacy Policy: https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • TikTok collects data according to their privacy practices
  • You can manage TikTok privacy settings in your TikTok account

4.3 Game Platforms

The games we feature (Minecraft, Roblox, Fortnite) have their own privacy policies:

  • Minecraft (Microsoft): https://privacy.microsoft.com/
  • Roblox: https://www.roblox.com/info/privacy
  • Fortnite (Epic Games): https://www.epicgames.com/site/en-US/privacypolicy

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

BlockBuddies4Life is committed to protecting children's privacy. Our content is designed for viewers aged 9-15, which includes children under 13.

5.1 Our Practices

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children
  • We do not have registration or login systems
  • We do not request personal information from viewers
  • Our website does not use cookies or tracking technologies

5.2 Platform Responsibility

YouTube and TikTok have their own policies and protections for children:

  • YouTube marks our content appropriately for child audiences
  • Platforms limit data collection and advertising for child viewers
  • Parents can use platform parental controls and restricted modes

5.3 Parental Rights

Parents who have concerns about their child's interaction with our content should:

  • Review and manage platform privacy settings
  • Use parental control features on YouTube and TikTok
  • Contact the relevant platform for data access or deletion requests
  • Contact us at contact@blockbuddies4life.com with any concerns

6. Data We Access for Content Creation

6.1 Analytics Data

We access aggregated, non-personally identifiable analytics from platforms to improve our content:

  • Video view counts and watch time
  • Engagement metrics (likes, shares, comments)
  • Geographic and demographic summaries (not individual data)
  • Content performance trends

This data is provided in aggregate form by the platforms and does not identify individual viewers.

6.2 Comments and Interactions

When viewers comment on our videos:

  • Comments are managed through platform systems
  • We may read and respond to comments using platform interfaces
  • Comment data is controlled by the platform, not by us
  • We moderate comments according to platform guidelines

7. Content Distribution

We distribute our content through official channels and APIs:

  • Videos are uploaded using platform-approved methods
  • We use TikTok API for automated content posting
  • No viewer data is transferred or shared as part of content distribution
  • Content metadata (titles, descriptions) does not contain personal information

8. Data Security

While we do not collect personal data directly:

  • Our API credentials and access tokens are securely stored
  • We use secure connections (HTTPS) for all platform communications
  • We follow platform best practices for API usage
  • We regularly review our security practices

9. Your Privacy Choices

9.1 On YouTube

  • Manage your YouTube history and privacy settings
  • Use YouTube's privacy controls in your Google account
  • Use Restricted Mode for additional content filtering
  • Control ad personalization settings

9.2 On TikTok

  • Adjust privacy settings in your TikTok account
  • Control who can view and interact with content
  • Manage data collection preferences
  • Use restricted mode if available

9.3 General

  • You can watch our content without creating accounts (on some platforms)
  • Browser privacy modes can limit tracking
  • VPN services can provide additional privacy

10. International Viewers

Our content is available globally. Privacy practices may vary by jurisdiction:

  • We comply with U.S. privacy laws including COPPA
  • Platform privacy policies apply based on viewer location
  • Some regions may have additional privacy protections (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
  • Contact platforms directly regarding jurisdiction-specific rights

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date." We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

12. Contact Information

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: contact@blockbuddies4life.com
  • Website: https://blockbuddies4life.com

13. Platform-Specific Privacy Resources

For specific privacy concerns or data requests, please contact the relevant platform:

  • YouTube/Google: https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/7671399
  • TikTok: https://support.tiktok.com/en/account-and-privacy

14. No Sale of Personal Information

BlockBuddies4Life does not sell, rent, or trade personal information because we do not collect personal information. We are a content creator, not a data broker.

15. Transparency Commitment

We are committed to transparency in our practices:

  • We clearly disclose our use of platform APIs
  • We direct users to platform privacy policies
  • We maintain family-friendly, age-appropriate content
  • We respond to privacy inquiries promptly
